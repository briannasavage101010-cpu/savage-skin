/ The 3-in-1

What it does.

One tube. Three jobs. A daily balm for everyday comfort and shine. An overnight mask when you layer it on before bed. And a hydration-plumper that plumps your lips with moisture — not a sting.

Most lip products are wax sticks that sit on top and quit by 10am. Lip Service is built like skincare: deep-hydration ingredients draw water into your lips so they look cushioned and feel soft, with a glassy, non-sticky finish.

Your lips are skin too. Treat them like it.

/ Full ingredients

Every ingredient, in plain English.

You lick your lips, you eat, you talk — lip products go with you all day. So here's what's in the tube and what each thing actually does. No mystery "fragrance," no fear list. Just the truth on the label.

  • Shea Butter Butyrospermum Parkii Butter
    Rich plant butter that seals and softens — the cushiony balm feel.
  • Squalane Squalane
    A weightless emollient, a lot like your skin's own oil. Locks moisture in without grease — the overnight-mask step.
  • Hyaluronic Acid Sodium Hyaluronate
    Draws water into your lips for a cushioned, hydrated plump — the fuller look comes from hydration, not a sting.
  • Glycerin Glycerin
    A humectant that holds soft, all-day hydration — the daily-balm step.
  • Vitamin E Tocopherol
    An antioxidant that conditions and protects lips against everyday wear.
