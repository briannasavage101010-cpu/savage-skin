Cookie Policy.

This explains how Savage Skin uses cookies and similar technologies on savageskincare.com, and how you can manage your choices.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your preferences, and — if you allow it — measure traffic and the performance of our ads.

How we use them

  • Essential — needed for the site to function, like keeping items in your cart. These are always on.
  • Analytics — help us understand how people use the site so we can improve it. Only set if you accept.
  • Marketing — used to measure and improve our advertising (for example, a Meta/Facebook pixel). Only set if you accept.

Managing your choices

When you first visit, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies in the banner at the bottom of the screen. You can change your mind anytime by clicking , or by clearing cookies in your browser settings.

A note for younger visitors

Savage Skin is intended for people aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly set non-essential or advertising cookies for visitors we know to be under 13. See our Privacy Policy for more.
