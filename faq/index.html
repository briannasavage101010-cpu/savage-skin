Are the formulas safe for younger / first-time routines?

Our formulas use real actives at real percentages, so we built them to be approachable even if you're new to actives — but acids and vitamin C still call for care. Patch-test before first use, introduce one product at a time, and always wear a broad-spectrum SPF during the day. If you have a skin condition or are unsure, check with a dermatologist first. Results vary, and this isn't medical advice.

Do I really need SPF with these?

Yes. Acids (in Prime Time Toner) and vitamin C (in Power Fix) can increase sun sensitivity, so daytime SPF isn't optional — it's part of the routine. Reapply through the day as directed on your sunscreen.

How do I use the full routine?

Cleanse twice daily with Clean Start. Tone with Prime Time 3x a week (PM). Treat with Power Fix in the AM, then always follow with SPF. Seal with Dew Guard in the PM. See each product page for full directions.
